How it works:

When Two-Factor Authentication is enabled, RevVault will require a one-time verification code sent to your registered email (user@example.com) whenever you sign in or perform sensitive operations.

We highly recommend enabling 2FA to keep your vault data extra safe.

Disabling 2FA reduces your account security.
